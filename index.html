Violin

Violin is a major component of classical music ensembles. It is the highest pitched instrument in the strings family. Learning violin is fun once you get a hang of it.

Guitar

Guitar is one of the most popular solo instruments. Playing the guitar is a form of therapy. It compliments well with vocals and can relax the player as well as the listener.

Piano

Playing the piano sharpens fine motor skills, improves dexterity and hand-eye coordination. It also makes your hands and arm muscles much stronger than the average person. The piano can accompany itself playing two parts simultaneously.

Vocals

While instruments are emotive and their musical qualities are great there is NOTHING like an amazing vocal. The singer tells the story and carries the melody. We offer both western vocals and Indian classical classes.

Drums

Playing the drums can be cathartic. While all forms of music can be therapeutic, getting to hit stuff is fun in itself. Drums set pace to the performance. You'll never go unnoticed with the drums.

Wind

Wind instruments form an important section of the classical orchestra. A range of wind instruments create a wide range of sound colors and believed to be a young instrument.
